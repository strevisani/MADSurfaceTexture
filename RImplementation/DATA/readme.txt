The digital terrain models of the Trentino Province have been derived via different post-elaborations from the Province of Trento cartographic service (“Ufficio Sistemi Informativi - Servizio autorizzazioni e valutazioni ambientali”) available at this link:
http://www.territorio.provincia.tn.it/portal/server.pt/community/lidar/847/lidar/23954
These are available with creative common license
Moreover, here we add also the synthetic surfaces used for testing the algorithms.
